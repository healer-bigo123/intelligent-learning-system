英语定语从句 (Attributive Clause)

一、定语从句的概念
定语从句是在复合句中修饰名词或代词的从句。被修饰的词叫先行词，引导定语从句的词叫关系词。

二、关系代词
1. who：指人，在从句中作主语
   The man who is standing there is my father.
2. whom：指人，在从句中作宾语
   The girl whom you met yesterday is my sister.
3. whose：指人或物，在从句中作定语
   The boy whose father is a doctor studies hard.
4. which：指物，在从句中作主语或宾语
   The book which is on the desk is mine.
5. that：指人或物，在从句中作主语或宾语
   The man that I talked to is Mr. Li.

三、关系副词
1. when：指时间，在从句中作时间状语
   I remember the day when I first came here.
2. where：指地点，在从句中作地点状语
   This is the school where I studied for three years.
3. why：指原因，在从句中作原因状语
   That is the reason why he was late.

四、只能用that的情况
1. 先行词被序数词或最高级修饰
   This is the best film that I have ever seen.
2. 先行词被all, every, no, some, few, little, much等修饰
   All that can be done has been done.
3. 先行词既有人又有物
   He talked about the things and people that he remembered.
4. 先行词被the only, the very, the same修饰
   This is the very book that I want.

五、定语从句的易错点
1. 介词+关系代词结构中不能用that
   The house in which we lived has been pulled down.
2. 非限制性定语从句不能用that引导
   My mother, who is a teacher, retired last year.
3. as和which引导非限制性定语从句的区别
   as可放在句首，which不可
   As is known to all, the earth is round.
